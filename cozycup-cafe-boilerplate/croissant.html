Croissant

Description: Croissant: A croissant is a buttery, flaky pastry with a delicate, crescent-shaped appearance. Originating from France, it's made from a laminated dough, which involves folding butter into the dough multiple times to create its signature layers. When baked, the croissant becomes light, airy, and crisp on the outside, with a soft, tender interior. It’s often enjoyed plain or with a variety of fillings like chocolate, almond, or ham and cheese.
